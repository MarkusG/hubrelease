curl to get github token:
curl --request POST \
  --url https://api.github.com/authorizations \
  -u "USERNAME" \
  --header 'Content-Type: application/json' \
  --header 'X-GitHub-OTP: OTP' \
  --data '{"scopes": ["public_repo"], "note": "test"}'
